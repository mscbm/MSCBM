团队成员

Hemi Luan

Professor

Professor Luan's research focuses on the technological development and transformational application of bioomics and informatics in complex environments - life and health. In recent years, he has been dedicated to the identification of toxic molecules in environmental exposure and the exploration of their health risk mechanisms, and has been actively promoting the cross-integration of advanced mass spectrometry analysis methods and artificial intelligence technologies.

About Us

Our team specializes in advanced mass spectrometry, intelligent algorithm development, and interdisciplinary platforms. Our research supports cutting-edge applications in food safety, environmental health, non-invasive disease diagnosis, and precision medicine.
